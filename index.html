HOW TO PLAY

Player 1 (Left)

WS Move up / down
Shift Hold to charge a power smash
D Activate Turbo (if drafted)
E Fire Supernova (if drafted)
W W / S S Double-tap to Dash

Player 2 / AI (Right)

↑↓ Move up / down
Enter Hold to charge a power smash
K Activate Turbo (if drafted)
I Fire Supernova (if drafted)
↑↑ / ↓↓ Double-tap to Dash

Extra Functions Mode

Spin: Moving your paddle while hitting adds spin — the ball curves mid-flight and reverses spin off walls.
Momentum: Hit while moving the same direction as the ball for up to +25% speed bonus.
Charge Shot: Hold Shift/Enter to fill the stamina bar. Release for a 2× speed smash. Stamina recharges over time.
Dash: Double-tap your up/down key to burst 1.5 paddle-lengths instantly. Costs 30 stamina, 0.5s cooldown.
Turbo (D / K): Temporarily boosts paddle speed and charge power. Pairs with Glass Cannon for a 2.5× Overdrive smash.
Supernova (E / I): Fires a shockwave that deflects the ball. Arms automatically after hitting; press to trigger.

Draft Mode Power-ups

Big Paddle: +25% paddle height per stack.
Glass Cannon: Shrinks your paddle but massively boosts charge shot speed.
Frenzy: Faster stamina regen, higher stamina cap.
Quick Feet: Increases paddle movement speed.
Magnetism: Ball curves toward your paddle when it's heading your way.
Blinder: Makes the ball semi-invisible to your opponent.
Ricochet: Hitting the ball spawns a ghost decoy that flies off at an angle.
Phantom Storm: Your ghost decoys are fully invisible — opponent can't tell which is real.
Rewind: If you concede a point, the ball snaps back to where it was ~1.5s ago. One use per point. More stacks = deeper rewind.
Mimic: Copies a random ability from your opponent's deck.
Sapper: Shrinks the opponent's paddle.
Curveball: Adds persistent spin to every ball you hit.
Time Warp: Slows the ball briefly after you hit it, giving you time to reposition.
Deep Freeze: Has a chance to freeze the opponent on each hit. Stacks increase duration and frequency.
Supernova / Turbo: See controls above. Both are draftable abilities.
